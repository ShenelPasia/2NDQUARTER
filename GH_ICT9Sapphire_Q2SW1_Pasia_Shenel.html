Dog Breeds

Poodle

 One of the smartest breeds in the world is the poodle, according to experts in the field. They can learn quickly and are excellent candidates for almost any work you provide them with.

Dachshund

 The little hunting hound known as the dachshund has a big personality.

Chihuahua

 Chihuahuas are intelligent, fiercely devoted to the people they love, and can travel everywhere with their owners due to their small size.
